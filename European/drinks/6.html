Drinks
This alcoholic beverage is produced with a base of a neutral spirit that is infused with a blend of botanicals, primarily the leaves of Artemisia absinthium, an ancient medicinal plant that is also known as wormwood or simply absinthe.
Meal prep time : 5 minutes
Servings : 1
Absinthe
Ingredients:
  • 1 ounce gin
  • 1 ounce lemon juice
  • 1 ounce Cointreau (or Grand Marnier)
  • 1 ounce dry vermouth or Lillet Blanc*
  • ½ teaspoon absinthe
  • For the garnish: Orange peel or orange wedge

Directions:
  1. Add the gin, lemon juice, Cointreau, dry vermouth, and absinthe to a cocktail shaker.
  2. Fill it with ice and shake it until cold.
  3. Strain into a cocktail glass. If desired, garnish with an orange peel or orange wedge.
