Drinks
Sometimes described as "Negroni’s long-lost autumnal cousin," Boulevardier is a mixed drink made with a combination of Campari, sweet vermouth, and bourbon or rye whiskey. The ingredients are mixed with ice, then strained into a chilled cocktail glass. The cocktail often garnished with a twist of orange peel or a cherry.
Meal prep time : 5 minutes
Servings : 1
Boulevardier
Ingredients:
  • 1 ounce bourbon or rye whiskey
  • 1 ounce campari
  • 1 ounce sweet vermouth
  • Garnish: orange twist or cherry

Directions:
  1. Pour ingredients into a mixing glass and fill with cracked ice.
  2. Stir well for 20 seconds and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  3. Garnish with a cherry or a twist of orange peel.
