Drinks
Negroni is a cocktail made with equal parts gin, sweet vermouth, and Campari. Although its origin is unclear, it is believed that this cocktail was invented in Florence in 1919 by Count Camillo Negroni, who wanted to improve his favorite drink - the Americano - by replacing soda with gin.
Meal prep time : 5 minutes
Servings : 1
Negroni
Ingredients:
  • 25ml Tanqueray Flor De Sevilla Distilled Gin
  • 25ml sweet Vermouth
  • 25ml Campari
  • Ice
  • Slice of orange

Directions:
  1. Pour the Tanqueray Flor De Sevilla Distilled Gin, Vermouth, and Campari into a mixing glass or jug with ice.
  2. Stir well until the outside of the glass feels cold.
  3. Strain into a tumbler and add 1 large ice sphere or some fresh ice, and garnish with an orange slice, using a blood orange when in season.
