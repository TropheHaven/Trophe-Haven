Drinks
Ouzo is one of the best-known Greek specialties whose large-scale production started in 1830. It is distilled from neutral alcohol that is diluted with water and flavored with a combination of aniseed and various botanicals, such as fennel seeds, coriander, cardamom, nutmeg, cinnamon, ginger, or angelica.
Meal prep time : 5 minutes
Servings : 1
Ouzo Cocktail
Ingredients:
  • 2 ½ ounces ouzo
  • ½ to 1 ounce fresh lemon juice, more if you prefer
  • 1 to 2 Fresh mint leaves, more for garnish
  • 1 teaspoon honey, agave, or simple syrup (optional)
  • Ice

Directions:
  1. In a mixing jar or cup, add the ouzo, lemon juice, and honey if using. Stir.
  2. Add the mint and muddle a bit (if you don’t have a muddler, you can use a small wooden spoon).
  3. Prepare a small glass and fill it with ice.
  4. Pour the ouzo cocktail over the ice.
  5. Garnish with fresh mint.
  6. Give the ouzo a quick swirl in the ice and enjoy.
