Breakfast & Brunch
Æggekage is a traditional Danish egg-based dish that resembles an omelet or a thick pancake. It consists of a mixture of lightly beaten eggs, milk, and flour, which is poured in a skillet and topped with various condiments. The dish is usually finished in the oven until the top is completely set and a light golden crust appears on the edges.
Meal prep time : 30 minutes
Servings : 4
Æggekage
Ingredients:
  • 10 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives
  • Coarse salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 4 slices of thick cut bacon or 6 regular slices of bacon, chopped into ½ inch pieces
  • 1 ½ cups cooked new potatoes cut into half-inch pieces.

Directions:
  1. Set oven rack so it is about 8 inches from the heating element. Turn on broiler.
  2. Whisk eggs, half and half, chives, ½ teaspoon salt and a few grinds of freshly ground pepper in a large bowl. Set aside.
  3. Fry bacon over medium heat in a 10 inch nonstick skillet until crisp.
  4. Using a slotted spoon, remove bacon from the skillet and drain on paper towels.
  5. Pour off all but 1 tablespoon of bacon fat from the pan.
  6. Add potatoes and cook until golden brown, about 3-5 minutes.
  7. Add reserved bacon and stir to combine.
  8. Pour egg mixture into the skillet.
  9. Using a rubber spatula and being sure to continuously scrape the bottom of the pan, stir eggs constantly until large curds begin to form, about 2-3 minutes.
  10. Shake the skillet and use the spatula to arrange eggs in an even layer in the pan, patting down the top (eggs will still appear quite wet at this point).
  11. Allow the eggs to cook without stirring for 30 seconds to set the bottom of the cake.
  12. Transfer the skillet to the oven and broil until surface is just beginning to brown, about 2 minutes.
  13. Carefully remove the skillet from the oven and let the egg cake rest for 5 minutes.
  14. Using a clean rubber spatula, loosen the cake from the sides of the pan and gently slide onto a serving plate.
  15. Sprinkle with additional fresh chives if desired. Serve.
