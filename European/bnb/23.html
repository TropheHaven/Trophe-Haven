Breakfast & Brunch
Potato scone are similar to potato pancakes. From an Irish and Scottish background, these scones are the perfect side with any breakfast meal. Potato scone are most often served with fried eggs, porridge, bacon, sliced sausage, they can alternatively be enjoyed with jam and a cup of tea.
Meal prep time : 25 minutes
Servings : 6
Potato Scone
Ingredients:
  • 1 pound cooked potatoes
  • 4 ounces self-rising flour
  • 2 ounces butter
  • ½ pinch salt

Directions:
  1. Gather all ingredients. Heat a lightly greased griddle or cast iron skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Mash potatoes with flour, butter, and salt in a large bowl until a stiff dough forms.
  3. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured work surface.
  4. Knead dough lightly and roll dough out to a 1/2-inch-thick circle.
  5. Cut into 6 equal wedges.
  6. Working in batches, cook scones on the hot griddle until golden brown, 4 to 5 minutes per side.
