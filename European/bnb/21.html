Breakfast & Brunch
This delicacy consists of a traditional flatbread (lepinja) that is cut in half, coated in thick cream (kajmak), and topped with an egg. The combination is shortly baked and should be doused with a mixture of warm meat drippings, which is locally known as pretop.
Meal prep time : 1 hour
Servings : 2
Komplet lepinja
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup flour
  • 125 ml water warm
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp sugar
  • 20 g fresh yeast
  • 1 tbsp old cream cheese or "kajmak"
  • 2 eggs
  • 6 tbsp roasting meat juice
  • 1.76 oz prosciutto

Directions:
  1. Add sugar and yeast to lukewarm water and let the yeast foam.
  2. Mix flour and salt, add water with yeast, oil and knead the dough.
  3. Leave it on a warm place 20 minutes.
  4. Then divide the dough into 2 parts, form balls and let it stand 10 minutes.
  5. Sprinkle the baking tray with flour, put balls of dough, form buns with your hands, sprinkle with oil and flour.
  6. Bake in a preheated oven at 392F (200°C) for about 15 minutes.
  7. When you take them out of the oven, cover them with a damp cloth to cool them down a bit.
  8. Take one bun and cut it across to get two pieces.
  9. Spread cream cheese on the lower part.
  10. Break one egg and stir directly on the lower part of the bun.
  11. Return to the oven for a few minutes or until the egg is cooked through.
  12. Remove from the oven and pour the bottom of the bun with a few tablespoons of roasting meat juice.
  13. Add the prosciutto and place the top of the bun.
