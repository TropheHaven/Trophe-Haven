Breakfast & Brunch
Smaženice is a popular Czech dish consisting of diced mushrooms that are braised with onions, seasoned with cumin, and combined with lightly beaten eggs. This rustic dish is commonly prepared in many Czech households, and it is usually served alongside bread and enjoyed as a quick snack.
Meal prep time : 30 minutes
Servings : 3
Smaženice
Ingredients:
  • 1 pound fresh mushrooms (450 g)
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 onions small, or 1 mid-sized yellow onion
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon caraway seeds crushed
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper ground
  • 3 Tablespoons water
  • 1 Tablespoon lard

Directions:
  1. Clean the mushrooms with a moist cloth, and scrape the top layer off the mushroom legs with a knife.
  2. Cut the mushrooms into larger chunks, about ¾ inch in size.
  3. If you find wormy spots, put these mushroom pieces aside; they do not belong in the smaženice.
  4. Peel and finely chop the onion.
  5. Heat the lard or oil in a heavy skillet over medium-high heat.
  6. Fry the onion in the pan until it starts to turn golden. Stir regularly.
  7. Now add the sliced mushrooms, season with salt, and crushed caraway seeds.
  8. Cook while stirring until the mushrooms begin to sweat. It takes about 5-6 minutes.
  9. Add about three tablespoons of water, cover with a lid, and let simmer for 5 minutes.
  10. Remove the lid, and reduce the heat to medium-low heat.
  11. Add the eggs, stir well and cook until they thicken.
  12. Scrape the bottom of the skillet periodically with a wooden spatula, stirring the eggs, until soft curds form.
  13. Don't let the eggs cook for long, so they don't dry out! The texture of the fry should be fine to creamy.
  14. Now remove the pan from the heat source, and season with pepper and salt to taste, if necessary.
