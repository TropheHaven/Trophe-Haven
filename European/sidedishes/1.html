Side Dishes
Andijviestamppot is a traditional, old-fashioned Dutch side dish consisting of mashed potatoes combined with mashed, raw endive and flavorful bits of bacon. The dish is additionally flavored with onions, vinegar, salt, and pepper, while milk is sometimes added for extra creaminess.
Meal prep time : 20 minutes
Servings : 4
Andijviestamppot (Dutch Escarole Mash)
Ingredients:
  • 400gr fresh escarole
  • 1,5 kg floury potatoes
  • 200 ml milk
  • 200gr bacon lardons
  • 1 large onion
  • 4 tbsp vinegar
  • pepper and salt to taste

Directions:
  1. Peel and rinse the potatoes and chop them into pieces. The bigger the pieces are, the longer it takes to cook them.
  2. Add the potatoes to the medium-sized pan and fill the pan with water and season with salt.
  3. Boil the potatoes for about 10 minutes, depending on their size.
  4. Meanwhile rinse the fresh escarole, dry it using the salad spinner and chop them into small pieces.
  5. Set the rinsed and chopped escarole aside and peel the onion.
  6. Slice the onion into small chunks and set aside.
  7. Heat up the medium-sized skillet and saute the bacon lardons without adding any extra oil or butter. Add the chopped onion after about 2 minutes and saute for a few minutes more. (the bacon needs to be crunchy)
  8. Drain the potatoes and mash them, you can use a potato masher or a fine sieve and a ladle to do this.
  9. The potatoes don't need to be super smooth, but there shouldn't be any big lumps left.
