Side Dishes
Fagioli all'uccelletto is an italian dish originating from Tuscany. It's made with a combination of cannellini beans, sage leaves, garlic, extra virgin olive oil, tomato paste, and fresh pork sausages. The beans and sausages are simmered over a low flame, then served with bread on the side.
Meal prep time : 14 minutes
Servings : 4
Fagioli all'uccelletto (Beans in Tomato Sauce)
Ingredients:
  • 14 oz canned cannellini beans (400g) drained
  • ½ cup crushed strained tomatoes (passata) (120g)
  • ½ cup water (120ml)
  • 5-6 sage leaves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to season

Directions:
  1. Add the olive oil to a frying pan or skillet on medium heat and add the chopped garlic and sage leaves.
  2. Fry for 1-2 minutes until the garlic is just starting to turn a light golden brown.
  3. Add the tomatoes followed by the water and a good pinch of salt and pepper, stir.
  4. Simmer the sauce for 5 minutes.
  5. After 5 minutes, add the beans and bring back to a simmer.
  6. Continue to cook for a further 5 minutes until the sauce has thickened slightly and the beans are warmed through, serve.
