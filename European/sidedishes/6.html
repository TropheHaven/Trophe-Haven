Side Dishes
Tumbet is a traditional Mallorcan vegetable dish consisting of baked layers of aubergines, red bell peppers, and potatoes which have previously been fried in olive oil. When served, tumbet is typically topped with a combination of fried tomatoes, garlic, and parsley.
Meal prep time : 1 hour 30 minutes
Servings : 4
Tumbet
Ingredients:
  • 2 medium aubergines, cut into 1.5cm/⅝in rounds
  • up to 200ml/7fl oz olive oil
  • 2 medium courgettes, cut into 2cm/¾in slices
  • 500g/1lb 2oz large waxy potatoes (such as Charlotte), thinly sliced (around 3mm/⅛in)
  • 2 roasted red peppers (from a jar), cut into strips
  • small bunch fresh oregano
  • small bunch fresh basil
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 400g/14oz ripe tomatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • pinch sugar (optional)

Directions:
  1. Preheat the oven to 200C/Fan 180C/Gas 6.
  2. Arrange the aubergine slices on a large baking tray and brush with olive oil.
  3. Season with salt. Roast for 20–25 minutes, or until softened and lightly browned.
  4. Remove from the oven and allow to cool.
  5. Pour a 5mm/¼in thick layer of olive oil in the base of a large, heavy-based frying pan.
  6. Fry the courgettes on a medium heat, for a couple of minutes on each side, then remove and drain onto kitchen towel. Season lightly with salt.
  7. Add the potato slices to the frying pan and fry on both sides until they are softened all the way through and a very light golden colour around the edges.
  8. This will take up to 10 minutes.
  9. Drain on a plate lined with kitchen paper.
  10. To make the sauce, put the olive oil in a saucepan and add the garlic.
  11. Cook for 3–4 minutes on a low–medium heat until soft and aromatic, then add the tomatoes and thyme. Season with salt and pepper.
  12. Bring to the boil, then reduce the heat and cook gently, uncovered, until well reduced – around 25 minutes.
  13. Taste after 10 minutes – if the sauce tastes acidic, add a pinch of sugar.
  14. Reduce the oven to 180C/160C Fan/Gas 4.
  15. To assemble, spread a couple of tbsp of tomato sauce in the base of an oven dish.
  16. Sprinkle over some fresh oregano and basil leaves.
  17. Top with all the aubergines.
  18. Add more tomato sauce, then sprinkle with more herbs and add the courgettes.
  19. Repeat with more tomato sauce, more herbs and the peppers, then finish with the last of the tomato sauce and herbs, and top with the potatoes.
  20. Sprinkle over more salt and pepper and drizzle with a little more olive oil.
  21. Bake in the oven for 25–30 minutes, or until the potatoes are crisp and browned and the vegetables underneath are tender.
