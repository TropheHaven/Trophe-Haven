Side Dishes
Rasstegai is a traditional Russian pastry with a hole in the center, which is stuffed with a wide variety of fillings – from liver with eggs to rice with mushrooms. However, the traditional stuffing is minced fish such as sturgeon or salmon.
Meal prep time : 2 hours 35 minutes
Servings : 8
Rasstegai
Ingredients:
  • 200 ml milk
  • 20 tbsp flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp dry yeast
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 egg
  • 2 onion
  • 700g minced meat
  • 70g butter
  • Spices to taste (dry)

Directions:
  1. Milk is heated to a temperature of 35 degrees.
  2. We breed yeast in it, add sugar, salt, sift the fourth part of the prepared and pre-sifted flour, and mix.
  3. The mixture should be thick like sour cream.
  4. Let the dough rise in a warm place for 20 minutes.
  5. Pour the remaining flour into the dough, which also needs to be sifted.
  6. Pour in the oil and knead a smooth and elastic dough.
  7. Form a ball, transfer it to a bowl.
  8. Cover with cling film and let stand in a warm place for 1 hour.
  9. Now we going to prepare the stuffing.
  10. Clean the onion and fry in a pan with the addition of butter until golden, stir.
  11. Add minced meat, salt, and your favorite spices to the onion.
  12. Also, fry the mass while stirring – do not over dry it.
  13. Approximate frying time – 7 minutes on moderate heat.
  14. Punch the dough, form a sausage, and divide it with a knife into equal pieces. The size of which will depend on the size of the rasstegai.
  15. Roll each piece into a thin cake and cut it from two edges.
  16. We put the filling in the center, pull off the cut edges, put one through the other and pinch from the sides. You will get half-open pies.
  17. Cover the baking sheet with baking paper, grease it with oil.
  18. Put rasstegai with meat on top.
  19. Lubricate them with an egg and let them stand for 20 minutes.
  20. Put the baking sheet in the oven, bake rasstegai for 30 minutes at 180 degrees.
  21. During this time, they will become brown and fully.
