Side Dishes
Urnebes is an authentic Serbian spread consisting of salty cheese, garlic, sour cream, roasted peppers, paprika, oil, and cooked egg yolks. It is traditionally served as a side dish accompanying numerous grilled meat dishes, although it can also be used as a topping for hamburgers or as a dip.
Meal prep time : 45 minutes
Servings : 3
Urnebes
Ingredients:
  • white cheese - 7 oz (200 g)
  • red peppers - 6 pcs.
  • olive oil - 2 tbsp.
  • hot paprika - 1/4 tsp.
  • garlic - 3 cloves

Directions:
  1. Roast the peppers.
  2. Peel them, clean them and put them in a small pot with a lid.
  3. Cut them into small pieces and blend them in a blender along with the garlic and white cheese.
  4. Add the olive oil and hot paprika to the homemade spread.
  5. Stir and leave the quick salad in the fridge for an hour.
  6. You can serve the Serbian Urnebes Salad as a side dish for meat or eat it as it is.
