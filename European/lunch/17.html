Lunches
This unique type of pizza is characterized by its half-round shape, made by folding a full-sized pizza in half. Typically, calzones are filled with meats such as salami or ham and cheeses such as mozzarella, ricotta, parmesan, and pecorino whereas fried calzones with mozzarella and tomatoes are a specialty from the Italian region of Apulia, and are known as panzerotti.
Meal prep time : 45 minutes
Servings : 1 (8 slices)
Calzone Pizza
Ingredients:
  • 2 lb pizza dough
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup salami
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Directions:
  1. Preheat the oven to 475 °F.
  2. Line the baking sheet with parchment paper or spray it with oil.
  3. Divide the dough into 8 equally sized pieces.
  4. Roll each piece into a 1/4" thick circle.
  5. Place the filling on half of each circle (pizza sauce, ricotta, pepperoni, salami and shredded mozzarella).
  6. Make sure to leave the edges clean to allow for sealing the calzone.
  7. Fold the side over the filling and crimp the edges.
  8. Place calzones on a baking sheet, leaving space apart.
  9. Cut a small vent on the top of each calzone.
  10. Brush the tops with oil and sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
  11. Bake for 15 minutes or until golden brown.
