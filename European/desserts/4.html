Desserts
These traditional Russian pancakes are prepared with flour, eggs, milk, yeast, and a pinch of salt. What distinguishes them from other pancakes is the addition of yeast, since similar equivalents mostly do not add any leavening agents. The batter is poured over a hot pan, shaped into a circle, and fried for a couple of minutes on both sides.
Meal prep time : 25 minutes
Servings : 5
Blini
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ¾ cup milk
  • 2 tbsps milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tbsp melted unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter

Directions:
  1. Combine flour, salt, and baking powder in a bowl.
  2. Whisk ¾ cup plus 2 tbsps milk, egg, and 1 tbsp melted butter together in a separate bowl.
  3. Mix into the flour mixture until the batter is fully combined.
  4. Heat 1 tbsp butter in a large skillet over medium-low heat.
  5. Cooking several blini at a time, drop batter, one tbsp at a time, onto the heated skillet.
  6. Cook until bubbles form, 1 ½ to 2 minutes.
  7. Flip and continue cooking until brown, about 1 minute more. Move to a paper towel-lined plate to help soak up excess butter.
  8. Repeat with the remaining batter.
