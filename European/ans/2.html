Appetizers & Snacks
Simple and elegant, crudités are traditional French appetizers consisting of raw vegetables which can be sliced or left intact. The vegetables typically used for crudités include carrots, celery, bell peppers, cucumbers, asparagus spears, broccoli, and cauliflower.
Meal prep time : 15 minutes
Servings : 20
Crudités
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup Broccoli
  • 1 cup Cauliflower
  • 1 cup Cucumber
  • 1 cup Radishes
  • 1 cup Mini Bell Peppers
  • 1 cup Celery
  • 1 cup Carrots
  • 1 cup Green Onions
  • 1 cup Shishito Peppers
  • 1 cup Endive
  • 1 cup Cherry Tomatoes
  • 1 cup Asparagus
  • 1 cup Green Beans
  • 1 cup Snap Peas
  • Hummus
  • Greek/Ranch Dip
  • Garlic Cheese Spread
  • Fresh Dill and Parsley

Directions:
  1. Arrange the vegetables on the platter, filling in the spaces around the dip (bowl).
  2. For a beautiful platter, separate veggies of like colors and use ones that are different in color and texture near each other.
  3. Enjoy with friends!
