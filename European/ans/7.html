Appetizers & Snacks
Gambas al Ajillo is a popular Spanish appetizer consisting of shrimps that are sautéed in a pan with minced garlic, lemon juice, paprika, and olive oil. The dish is usually consumed as a tapa. It is recommended to garnish gambas al ajillo with chopped parsley and pair it with some crusty bread on the side, which is useful for mopping up the flavorful sauce.
Meal prep time : 30 minutes
Servings : 2
Gambas al Ajillo
Ingredients:
  • 12 cloves garlic
  • 1pound large shrimp, peeled, shells reserved
  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • Kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch red pepper flakes, or a 1-inch piece dried guajillo chile
  • 1 1/2 tsp sherry vinegar
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley leaves

Directions:
  1. Finely mince 4 garlic cloves and place in large bowl.
  2. Smash 4 cloves under the flat side of a knife and place in a large skillet.
  3. Thinly slice remaining four garlic cloves and set aside.
  4. Add shrimp to bowl with minced garlic.
  5. Add 3 tbsp olive oil, 3/4 tsp kosher salt, and baking soda.
  6. Toss to combine thoroughly and set aside at room temperature.
  7. Add shrimp shells to skillet with smashed garlic and add remaining olive oil and pepper flakes.
  8. Set over medium-low to low heat and cook, stirring and tossing occasionally, until shells are deep ruby red, garlic is pale golden brown, and oil is intensely aromatic, about 10 minutes.
  9. Oil should be gently bubbling the whole time.
  10. When ready, strain through a fine-mesh strainer into a small bowl, tossing and pressing the shrimp shells to extract as much oil as possible.
  11. Discard shells and garlic.
  12. Return flavored oil to skillet and heat over medium-high heat until shimmering.
  13. Add sliced garlic and cook, stirring constantly, until pale golden brown, about 1 minute.
  14. Add shrimp and cook, tossing and stirring constantly until shrimp are barely cooked through, about 2 minutes.
  15. Add sherry vinegar and parsley and toss to combine.
  16. Season to taste with salt.
  17. Serve immediately.
