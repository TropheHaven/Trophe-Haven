Appetizers & Snacks
This aromatic, decadent spread is made with black or green olives. The original recipe includes four essential Mediterranean ingredients – chopped olives, anchovies, capers, and olive. French restaurants usually serve it as an appetizer with a warm, crunchy baguette on the side. Sometimes, it is also used as a dipping sauce for crackers, assorted vegetables, or breadsticks.
Meal prep time : 10 minutes
Servings : 1
Tapenade
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup Castelvetrano olives, pitted*
  • ½ cup Niçoise or Kalamata olives, pitted*
  • ¼ cup lightly packed fresh flat-leaf parsley (thin stems are ok)
  • 1 tbsp drained capers
  • ¼ cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 medium cloves garlic, pressed or minced
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Directions:
  1. In the bowl of your food processor, combine all of the ingredients (pitted olives, parsley, capers, olive oil, garlic and lemon juice).
  2. Pulse briefly about 10 times, then scrape down the sides of the jar.
  3. Pulse 5 to 10 more times until well chopped, but not pureed, or until you reach your desired texture.
  4. Serve as desired.
  5. Store leftovers in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
