Appetizers & Snacks
The signature of vol-au-vent was known as the financière, the filling of which consisted of minced chicken, breadcrumbs, and mushrooms in a Madeira sauce. Today, vol-au-vent is typically served as a snack or an appetizer with either a chicken or fish filling. There is also a Belgian variety of vol-au-vent that can be found in most restaurants, filled with a combination of chicken, mushrooms, and tiny meatballs and served with a side of french fries or mashed potatoes.
Meal prep time : 40 minutes
Servings : 6
Vol-au-vent
Ingredients:
  • 1 package (17.30 ounces) frozen puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 6 bacon strips
  • 2 medium leeks (white parts only), sliced
  • 1 medium sweet yellow pepper, diced
  • 1 cup shredded rotisserie chicken
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • Minced fresh parsley plus additional ground pepper

Directions:
  1. Preheat oven to 400°.
  2. On a lightly floured surface, unfold 1 puff pastry sheet.
  3. Using a 3-1/4-in. round cutter, cut out 6 circles.
  4. Place on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  5. Unfold the remaining pastry sheet.
  6. Cut 6 more circles out with the 3-1/4-in. round cutter; with a 2-1/2-in. cutter, cut centers out of the circles.
  7. Place the rings on top of the circles that are on the baking sheet.
  8. Place the center circles also on the baking sheet.
  9. In a small bowl, whisk egg and water; brush over pastries.
  10. Chill 15 minutes.
  11. Bake until dark golden brown, 20-25 minutes.
  12. Cool on a wire rack.
  13. Meanwhile, in a large skillet, cook bacon over medium heat until crisp.
  14. Remove to paper towels to drain.
  15. Discard all but 1 tablespoon drippings.
  16. Add leeks and pepper to drippings; cook and stir over medium-high heat until tender, 5-7 minutes.
  17. Reduce heat to low; stir in bacon, chicken, cream cheese, salt and pepper.
  18. Cook and stir until blended; remove from heat.
  19. When cool enough to handle, hollow out pastries with a small knife.
  20. Fill with chicken mixture.
  21. Sprinkle with parsley and pepper.
  22. Serve with small center pastries on the side.
