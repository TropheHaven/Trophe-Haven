Breakfasts & Brunches
Gözleme is a Turkish flatbread consisting of flour, water, yeast, olive oil, and yogurt, which prevents the flatbread from going too brittle. The dough is filled with Ingredients such as meat, vegetables, eggs, various cheeses, or mushrooms, and is then baked on a sac griddle.
Meal prep time : 45 minutes
Servings : 2
Gözleme
Ingredients:
    For The Dough
  • 1 cup flour
  • a little bit less than 1/2 cup spring water, room temperature
  • 1/2 tsp salt

    • Parsley Filling
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese (or, beyaz peynir), crumbled
  • 1/4 cup parsley, chopped

    • Glaze
  • 1 tbsp butter, melted

    • Meat Filling
  • 150 g lean ground beef (or lamb)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • parsley, chopped

    • Spinach Filling
  • 150 g frozen chopped spinach, defrosted
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1 pinch crushed pepper
  • salt and pepper, to taste

    • Potato Filling
  • 1 yellow potato, boiled, peeled, mashed
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 ½ tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1 pinch crushed pepper
  • salt and pepper, to taste

    • Roasted Eggplant Filling
  • 1 medium sized eggplant, roasted, peeled, drained
  • 1/4 cup kassari or mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 pinch crushed pepper
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:
  1. Mix the flour and the salt in a bowl, then gradually add the water, kneading as your pour until you have a dough. Turn the dough onto a lightly floured working surface and knead for 10 minutes. Cover with a damp cloth and let the dough rest like that for 15 minutes.
  2. Divide the dough into four same-sized pieces, then roll out each piece until 25cm in diameter.
  3. Mix the feta and the parsley and arrange the filling in the middle then fold the two opposite sides, then the second two opposite sides.
  4. Warm a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  5. Place one gözleme in the pan and cook it until blistered with brown spots, then turn over, and brush with melted butter.
  6. Once cooked on one side, flip it and brush the top with butter.
  7. When cooked on the other side as well, brush that side with melted butter as well.
  8. Serve warm, with a glass of ayran.
  9. To make the meat filling, cook all the Ingredients, then finish with a sprinkle of chopped parsley.
  10. For the spinach filling, first, sweat the onions in olive oil, then add all the Ingredients except feta and sauté for 4-5 minutes. Take off heat and stir in the feta.
  11. For the potato filling, first, sweat the onion in olive oil, then add in the remaining Ingredients.
  12. To make the roasted eggplant filling, combine all the ingredients.
