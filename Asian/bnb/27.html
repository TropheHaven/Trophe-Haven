Breakfasts & Brunches
Gado-gado is an Indonesian version of a mixed salad. It usually consists of a variety of vegetables, eggs, tempeh, and tofu. The vegetables are usually just slightly boiled, tossed with a nutty sauce, then completed with the addition of crispy prawn crackers.
Meal prep time : 30 minutes
Servings : 2
Gado – Gado
Ingredients:
  • Cabbage
  • Spinach
  • Bean sprouts
  • Snake beans or green beans
  • Lettuce
  • Cucumber
  • Grape tomatoes
  • Potato - steamed
  • Tempeh & Tofu
  • 1-2 boiled eggs
  • 2 tablespoon peanut sauce or satay sauce

Directions:
  1. Steam all vegetables except lettuce, cucumber and tomato.
  2. Spray a non-stick pan, lightly fry the tofu & tempeh
  3. Mix all vegetables, tofu, tempeh and egg, top with peanut sauce.
