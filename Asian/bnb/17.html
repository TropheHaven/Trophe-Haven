Breakfasts & Brunches
This traditional Turkish dish combines coarsely-ground cornmeal and (oftentimes) aged local cheeses such as trabzon or kashar. Grated cheese is mixed into piping hot cornmeal until it turns stringy, and the dish is then typically enriched with butter or kaymak—Turkish clotted cream.
Meal prep time : 15 minutes
Servings : 4
Kuymak
Ingredients:
  • 2 tablespoons of butter,
  • 2 tablespoons of cornmeal,
  • 2 cups of warm water,
  • 150 g of cheese (any melting cheese such as cheddar, provolone, swiss cheese...),
  • Salt.

Directions:
  1. Melt the butter in a saucepan,
  2. Add the corn meal and stir for 1-2 mins over medium heat,
  3. Add the water and stir really well,
  4. Now add the grated cheese and cook until the cheese melts and combines with the water and butter,
  5. Serve immediately.
