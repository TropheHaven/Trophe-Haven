Drinks
Lapsang Souchong is a type of black tea that originates from the Wuyi Mountains region of Fujian in China. It's sometimes labelled as smoked tea and rightfully so because the tea leaves are smoke-dried over a pinewood fire. It is believed that Lapsang Souchong originated from the Qing dynasty.
Meal prep time : 5 minutes
Servings : 1
Lapsang Souchang
Ingredients:
  • 1 1/2 fl oz Lapsang Souchong
  • 1 jigger (1.5 fl oz) Hendricks Gin
  • 1/2 can or bottle (12 fl oz) tonic water
  • 2 sprigs of thyme

Directions:
  1. Steep your lapsang souchong tea for 5 minutes then let it cool. You will only need 1.5 fl oz (40 ml) of your tea. You can refrigerate the rest for more lapsang souchong cocktails.
  2. Add ice to a cocktail glass.
  3. Add 1.5 fl oz (40 ml) of cold lapsang souchong tea, 1.5 fl oz (40 ml) of Hendricks Gin and fill the rest of the glass with tonic water.
  4. Stir gently and decorate with fresh thyme.
