Drinks
Genmaicha is a mix of roasted green tea (bancha or sencha) and roasted brown rice (genmai), though white rice is also sometimes used. Invented in Kyoto by a local merchant, genmaicha was initially regarded as a standard and cheap tea option.
Meal prep time : 11 minutes
Servings : 4
Genmaicha
Ingredients:
  • 2 tbsp brown rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 4 tsp green tea leaves

Directions:
  1. Put the rice in a small skillet and toast over medium-low heat until it turns dark in spots. Move to a small saucepan.
  2. Pour in the water and bring to a boil. Immediately reduce the heat to low, then cover and simmer for 1 minute.
  3. Remove from heat and allow to steep for 3 minutes more. Add the tea and let it steep for another 3 minutes.
  4. Strain and discard the rice and tea leaves from the liquid.
  5. Serve the tea hot and enjoy.
