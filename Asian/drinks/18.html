Drinks
Hong Kong milk tea is a beverage with a creamy and smooth texture, made with a combination of water, black tea leaves, and either sweetened condensed milk or evaporated milk and sugar. The drink is called milk tea in order to distinguish it from Chinese tea, which is typically served plain.
Meal prep time : 9 minutes
Servings : 4-6
Hong Kong-style milk tea
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups water
  • 4 tablespoons black tea leaves, preferably a bold Ceylon tea
  • 1 (14-ounce) can sweetened condensed milk, or to preferred taste

Directions:
  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Combine the water and tea leaves in a small saucepan ove medium heat.
  3. Bring to a low boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for 3 minutes
  4. Remove from the heat. Stir in the sweetened condensed milk to taste. Return to the heat.
  5. Bring back to a boil. Simmer for 3 more minutes.
  6. Strain the tea and condensed milk mixture.
  7. Serve hot or chill and serve over ice. Small glasses are ideal.
