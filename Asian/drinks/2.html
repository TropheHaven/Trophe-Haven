Drinks
The Ginger Rogers is the perfect poolside cocktail. It’s refreshing and it’s easy to make. Not to mention mint, gin, ginger, and ginger ale are perfect compliments. Ideally you’d want to use a collins glass for this cocktail, but when you’re poolside you’ll take what you can get.
Meal prep time : 5 minutes
Servings : 1
Ginger Rogers
Ingredients:
  • 8 to 10 mint leaves
  • ¾ ounce ginger syrup
  • 2 ounce London dry gin
  • 1 ounce lemon juice, fresh squeezed
  • Ginger ale, chilled, to top (about 2 ounces)
  • Garnish: mint sprig.

Directions:
  1. Add the mint leaves and ginger syrup into a Collins glass and gently muddle.
  2. Add the gin, lemon juice and ice.
  3. Add the gin, lemon juice and ice.
  4. Garnish with a mint sprig.
