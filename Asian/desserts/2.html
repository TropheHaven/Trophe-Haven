Desserts
Gulab jamun is a traditional indian dessert and commonly available sweet in any indian restuarant everywhere. Usually many make them using ready mixes but they contain plain flour/wheat flour and Full fat Milk as well milk powder. They need to be deep fried in slow flame then put in Sugar syrup.
Meal prep time : 1 hour 5 minutes
Servings : 6
Gulab Jamun in Honey and Saffron Syrup
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup (150g) full-cream milk powder
  • 1/2 cup (75g) self-rising flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) milk
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) thin cream
  • Vegetable oil, to deep-fry
  • Pistachio nuts, finely chopped, to serve

    • Honey saffron syrup:
  • 1 cup (250ml) hot water
  • 1/2 tsp saffron threads
  • 1/2 cup (100g) caster sugar
  • 3 cardamom pods, bruised
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1/2 cup (125ml) honey

Directions:
  1. To make the syrup, combine the water and saffron in a saucepan.
  2. Set aside for 5 minutes to soak. Add sugar, cardamom and cinnamon; place over low heat.
  3. Cook, stirring, for 1-2 minutes or until sugar dissolves.
  4. Add honey; stir to combine.
  5. Bring to a simmer and cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes or until syrup thickens slightly.
  6. Meanwhile, combine milk powder, flour, baking powder, cinnamon and cardamom in a bowl and make a well in the center.
  7. Pour milk and cream into the well and stir until a soft and slightly sticky dough forms.
  8. Roll heaped teaspoonfuls of dough into balls; place on a plate.
  9. Heat enough oil in a saucepan over medium-high heat to 170°C (to test when oil is ready, a cube of bread turns golden brown in 20 seconds).
  10. Place one-quarter of the dumplings in hot oil and cook, turning occasionally, for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown and cooked through.
  11. Use a slotted spoon to transfer to a plate lined with paper towel.
  12. Repeat in 3 more batches with remaining dumplings.
  13. Place the dumplings in the syrup and set aside for 30 minutes to soak.
  14. Spoon evenly among serving bowls.
  15. Sprinkle with pistachios to serve.
