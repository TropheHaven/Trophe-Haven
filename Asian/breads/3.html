Breads
Japanese milk bread, also known as "Hokkaido milk bread" or "shokupan," is a soft and fluffy white bread that is popular in Japan. It has a slightly sweet flavor and a tender crumb, making it a favorite for sandwiches and toast. The key to its unique texture is the use of a method called tangzhong, where a portion of the flour is cooked with water or milk into a thick, roux-like paste before being incorporated into the dough.
Meal prep time : 4 hours
Servings : 16
Japanese Milk Bread
Ingredients:
    Tangzhong:
  • 1/3 cup (40g) bread flour
  • 1 cup (240ml) whole milk

    • Bread Dough:
  • 2 1/2 cups (315g) bread flour
  • 1/4 cup (50g) sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) whole milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup (60g) unsalted butter, softened

Directions:
    Tangzhong:
  1. In a small saucepan, whisk together the flour and milk until there are no lumps.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens into a pudding-like consistency.
  3. Remove from heat and let it cool to room temperature.
    Bread Dough:
  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the bread flour, sugar, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the yeast and warm milk. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  3. Add the yeast mixture, tangzhong, egg, and softened butter to the dry ingredients.
  4. Mix until a dough forms, then knead for about 10-15 minutes until the dough becomes smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a lightly greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours or until it has doubled in size.
  6. Punch down the dough, divide it into equal portions, and shape each portion into a ball.
  7. Place the balls in a greased loaf pan, cover with a damp cloth, and let them rise for another 45 minutes to 1 hour.
  8. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  9. Bake the bread for 25-30 minutes or until the top is golden brown.
  10. Allow the bread to cool in the pan for a few minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.
