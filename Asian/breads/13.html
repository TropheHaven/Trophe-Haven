Breads
Korean garlic cheese bread is a popular street food in South Korea that combines the flavors of garlic, butter, and cheese in a delightful way. It typically involves a baguette or other types of bread filled with a mixture of garlic, butter, and cream cheese, then topped with additional cheese and baked until golden and gooey.
Meal prep time : 50 minutes
Servings : 4-6
Korean Garlic Cheese Bread
Ingredients:
  • Garlic Butter and Cream Cheese Spread:
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

    • Other Ingredients:
  • 1 long baguette or bread of your choice
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded (enough to sprinkle on top)
  • Additional fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Directions:
  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Prepare the Garlic Butter and Cream Cheese Spread:
  3. In a bowl, combine the softened butter, cream cheese, minced garlic, chopped parsley, and salt. Mix until well combined. You can use a hand mixer or a fork for this.
  4. Cut the baguette into individual portions, leaving one side attached, creating a pocket to hold the filling.
  5. Spread the garlic butter and cream cheese mixture generously inside the pockets of the bread.
  6. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the garlic butter and cream cheese mixture.
  7. Place the filled bread on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the bread is toasted.
  8. Optionally, garnish with additional fresh parsley for a pop of color and added flavor.
  9. Serve the Korean garlic cheese bread while it's warm. Enjoy the gooey, cheesy, and garlic-infused goodness!
