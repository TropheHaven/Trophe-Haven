Masala Papad is a popular Indian snack made from a thin, crispy flatbread called papad. It is usually served as an appetizer or side dish. The papad is topped with a mixture of spices, onions, tomatoes, and other ingredients. It is usually served with chutney or yogurt. Masala Papad is a popular snack in India.
Meal prep time : 10 minutes
Servings : 6
Masala Papad
Ingredients:
  • 12 papads
  • ¼ red onion finely chopped
  • 1 large tomato finely chopped
  • ¼ cup cilantro minced
  • 1 medium cucumber finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoon chat masala
  • 2 teaspoon cayenne
  • 1 lime
  • 3 Thai green chillies minced, optional
  • salt to taste

Directions:
  1. Cook the papad - my preferred method is roasting on an open flame. The little char it gets on the edges gives it a unique flavor. Keep flipping the papads over the flame with a pair of tongs, till all of the papad is cooked. There will be a few charred spots, but that is part of the game.
  2. Get all the toppings together, ready to go.
  3. Lay out the papads next to each other. Add a little of each topping to each of the papads. They are now ready to eat!
