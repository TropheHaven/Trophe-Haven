Side Dishes
Caramelized apples and onions made a delicious side dish for just about any meal back in the pioneer days. It had the sweetness of the onions, combined with the tartness of the apples
Meal prep time : 25 minutes
Servings : 4
Fried Onions and Apples
Ingredients:
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 4 apples - peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 1 onion, cut into matchstick-sized pieces
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon, or to taste (Optional)

Directions:
  1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook and stir apples, onion, brown sugar, and cinnamon in the melted butter until onions are translucent and sauce is slightly reduced, 15 to 20 minutes.
  3. Cool before serving.
