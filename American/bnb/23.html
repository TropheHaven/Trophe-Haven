Breakfasts & Brunches
Hot turkey sandwich is a traditional American open-faced sandwich that's heavily associated with Thanksgiving. It's typically made after Thanksgiving with leftovers such as sliced turkey meat, turkey gravy, stuffing, and cranberry relish. The ingredients are simply placed on top of a slice of bread and served.
Meal prep time : 10 minutes
Servings : 4
Hot Turkey Sandwich
Ingredients:
  • 1 ½ pound leftover turkey meat, sliced
  • ½ cup or so leftover turkey gravy
  • Stock or water (to thin the gravy if needed)
  • 4 large slices rustic bread

Directions:
  1. Heat gravy in a skillet until bubbly, if the gravy is too thick, thin with a little stock or water.
  2. Add pieces of sliced cooked turkey meat to the gravy, coat with the gravy on all sides and heat until the meat is heated through.
  3. Use Rustic French or Italian loaf would work great.
  4. Top with sliced turkey and gravy.
