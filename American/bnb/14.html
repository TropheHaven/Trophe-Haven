Breakfasts & Brunches
Calentado is a traditional Colombian breakfast consisting of last night's leftovers that are simply reheated. It stems from the past, when much of the Colombian population lived in poverty and nothing was wasted. This hearty breakfast usually includes rice, beans, plantains, steak, fried eggs, and arepas.
Meal prep time : 25 minutes
Servings : 4
Calentado
Ingredients:
  • 2 tbsps vegetable oil
  • 1 cup tomato, chopped
  • ½ cup green onions, chopped
  • 2 cups Colombian beans, cooked
  • 1 cup white rice, cooked
  • Colombian arepa, to taste
  • Fried or scrambled eggs, to taste

Directions:
  1. Heat the oil in a large frying pan on medium heat and add tomato and onion.
  2. Cook for 5 minutes, add beans and rice.
  3. Stir until all the ingredients are well mixed and cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Serve immediately with arepas and eggs.
