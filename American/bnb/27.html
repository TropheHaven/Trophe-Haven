Breakfasts & Brunches
Avocado toast is a dish consisting of a piece of toasted bread topped with a combination of mashed avocados, salt, pepper, and (sometimes) citrus juice. There are many varieties of this dish, so it can be enriched with ingredients such as salmon, tomatoes, onions, eggs, garlic, cheese, olive oil, or red pepper flakes.
Meal prep time : 5 minutes
Servings : 1
Avocado Toast
Ingredients:
  • 1 slice of bread (I like thick-sliced whole-grain bread best)
  • ½ ripe avocado
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional: Any extra toppings

Directions:
  1. Toast your slice of bread until golden and firm.
  2. Remove the pit from your avocado.
  3. Use a big spoon to scoop out the flesh.
  4. Put it in a bowl and mash it up with a fork until it’s as smooth as you like it.
  5. Mix in a pinch of salt (about ⅛ tsp) and add more to taste, if desired.
  6. Spread avocado on top of your toast. Enjoy as-it-is or top with any extras.
