Breakfasts & Brunches
A staple of American childhood foods, peanut butter and jelly sandwich, or PB&J for short is a sandwich consisting of two slices of bread, one with a layer of peanut butter, and the other one with jelly or jam. Quick, easy, and affordable, the first written reference for the sandwich appeared in 1901 in the Boston Cooking School Magazine of Culinary Science and Domestic Economics.
Meal prep time : 15 minutes
Servings : 1
Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich
Ingredients:
  • 2 tsp butter
  • 2 slices white bread
  • 2 tsp any flavor fruit jelly
  • 1 tsp peanut butter

Directions:
  1. Heat a griddle or skillet to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Spread jelly on the unbuttered side of one slice of bread; spread peanut butter on the other unbuttered side.
  4. Place bread slices together, so peanut butter and jelly are in the middle.
  5. Cook on the preheated griddle until golden brown and heated through, about 4 minutes per side.
