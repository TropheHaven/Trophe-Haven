Appetizers & Snacks
Beggar's purse is an American bite-sized appetizer originating from New York City. The appetizer consists of a small crêpe that's topped with a tsp of fine caviar and a dab of crème fraiche. The edges of the small crêpe are pulled up around the filling, then tied with strips of chive in order to resemble a purse.
Meal prep time : 1 hour 15 minutes
Servings : 8
Beggar's Purse
Ingredients:
    For crêpes:
  • 5 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp chopped chives

    • For purses:
  • 16 to 20 long chives (from 1 bunch)
  • 3/4 cup sour cream (preferably at room temperature)
  • 4 ounces domestic caviar such as osetra or rainbow trout

Directions:
  1. Melt butter in a 10-inch nonstick skillet over medium heat and set aside 2 tbsp for cooking crêpes. Continue to cook butter remaining in skillet until it has a nutty fragrance and is golden brown, about 2 minutes.
  2. Blend brown butter with remaining crêpe ingredients, except chives, in a blender until smooth. Add chives and pulse 1 or 2 times to just combine. Let batter stand 30 minutes.
  3. Blend brown butter with remaining crêpe ingredients, except chives, in a blender until smooth. Add chives and pulse 1 or 2 times to just combine. Let batter stand 30 minutes.
  4. Blanch chives in a small saucepan of boiling water 10 seconds. Drain and plunge into an ice bath. Pat dry.
    Make crêpes:
  1. Lightly brush skillet with butter, then heat over medium-high heat until hot but not smoking. Stir batter.
  2. With skillet off heat, add a scant ¼ cup batter, tilting and rotating skillet to coat bottom. (If batter sets too fast, reduce heat slightly.)
  3. Cook until golden around edges, 15 to 30 seconds. Loosen with a heatproof plastic spatula, then flip crêpe over with your fingertips. Cook until underside is set, about 30 seconds.
  4. Slide onto a plate. Make more crêpes, brushing skillet with butter and stacking crêpes on plate.
  5. Top each crêpe, browned side up, with about 1 ½ tbsp sour cream and 2 tsp caviar.
  6. Gather crêpe around filling and tie 1 or 2 blanched chives in a knot to close purse. Trim ends of chives if necessary.
