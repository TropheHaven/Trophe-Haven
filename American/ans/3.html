Appetizers & Snacks
Tuna tartare is an elegant starter originating from Los Angeles. The dish is made with a combination of raw tuna, avocado, egg yolks, green peppercorns, capers, chives, tarragon, mustard, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Nowadays, it's usually served as a visually attractive appetizer with crackers or toast.
Meal prep time : 15 minutes
Servings : 8
Tuna Tartare
Ingredients:
  • 1 lb fresh tuna (sushi grade)
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1 ½ tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 ½ tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 1 large green onion finely sliced

Directions:
  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the ginger, soy sauce, lime juice and sesame oil until combined.
  2. Pour the sauce over the tuna and gently mix until incorporated.
  3. Transfer into serving bowls and sprinkle with the chopped chives and toasted sesame seeds.
