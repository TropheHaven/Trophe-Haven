Appetizers & Snacks
Bacon Wrapped Shrimp is cooked in the oven with brown sugar for a sweet and savory appetizer! This recipe is so quick and easy to make. It’s perfect for any party, from New Year’s Eve to game day.
Meal prep time : 30 minutes
Servings : 10
Bacon Wrapped Shrimp
Ingredients:
  • 1 lb shrimp (cleaned, peeled, and deveined)
  • 7 thick pieces of bacon cut into thirds
  • 4 tbsps salted butter melted
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • ½ tbsp chipotle powder

Directions:
  1. Line a baking sheet with foil. Preheat oven to 400F.
  2. Wrap each piece of shrimp with ⅓ slice of bacon and secure with a toothpick. Place on the baking sheet.
  3. Mix together the melted butter, brown sugar, and chipotle powder.
  4. Brush each shrimp liberally with the butter mixture, using about 2/3 of it. Save the leftover butter mixture and set aside.
  5. Bake for 15 minutes. Take the shrimp out of the oven and switch to broil.
  6. Brush each shrimp one more time with the remaining butter mixture (stir before brushing).
  7. Broil for 1-2 minutes or until bubbly.
