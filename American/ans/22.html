Appetizers & Snacks
Chocolate covered potato chips is an American invention that is commonly consumed as a dessert snack. Just like the name suggests, these snacks consist of thin potato chips that are simply dipped in melted chocolate. The chocolate may be regular milk chocolate, dark chocolate, or white chocolate, and the chips are typically ridged because they can hold more chocolate than the regular, straight-cut variety.
Meal prep time : 20 minutes
Servings : 12
Chocolate Covered Potato Chips
Ingredients:
  • 24 ounces milk chocolate chips
  • 2 tbsps vegetable shortening
  • 4.5 ounce bag of ridged potato chips

Directions:
  1. Line two baking sheets with parchment paper; set aside.
  2. In a medium heatproof bowl set over a small saucepan of barely simmering water, combine the chocolate chips and vegetable shortening, stirring occasionally, until completely melted and smooth.
  3. Drop a few potato chips at a time into the bowl and, using two forks, flip the potato chips over so they are completely coated in chocolate.
  4. Lift out of the chocolate, allowing any excess to drip back into the bowl, and place on the lined baking sheets. Repeat until you have used up all of the potato chips and/or chocolate.
  5. Allow to set at room temperature until the chocolate is firm, about 2 hours.
