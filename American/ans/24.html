Appetizers & Snacks
Sincronizadas are Mexican snacks consisting of two corn or flour tortillas, cheese, and slices of ham. Optionally, beans and chorizo sausage can also be added to the dish as a filling. Quesadillas sincronizadas are commonly cut into wedges and served with guacamole, pico de gallo, or sour cream.
Meal prep time : 10 minutes
Servings : 4
Sincronizadas
Ingredients:
  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 slices good quality ham
  • 4 slices white or yellow cheddar, or cheese of your choice
  • Salsa
  • Guacamole

Directions:
  1. Lay out four of the corn tortillas and top with a slice of ham and cheese and spread some salsa over it. Cover with a second tortilla.
  2. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat and place the finished tortillas in it.
  3. Fry until the cheese is melted and the corn tortilla is lightly freckled with golden brown.
  4. Transfer to a warmed plate and repeat with the remaining sinchronizadas.
  5. Serve with guacamole.
