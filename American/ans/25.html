Appetizers & Snacks
A staple of Southern fried food in the United States, hushpuppies are an extremely popular snack consisting of deep-fried cornmeal batter. The batter is sometimes enriched by the addition of onions, garlic, corn, and peppers. Hushpuppies are best when served steaming hot with tartar sauce or a smoked trout dip.
Meal prep time : 20 minutes
Servings : 8
Hushpuppy
Ingredients:
  • oil For frying
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 3/4 cup Cornmeal
  • 1 Tbsp Sugar
  • 1 ½ tsp Baking Powder
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • ½ tsp Garlic Powder
  • ½ tsp Onion Powder
  • ½ tsp Paprika
  • ¼ tsp Pepper
  • 1 Egg Large
  • 1 cup Buttermilk
  • 4 tbsps Butter Cold and cut into small cubes
  • Tartar Sauce

Directions:
  1. Preheat 2 inches of oil in a large skillet to 365°.
  2. In a medium sized mixing bowl add the flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking powder, salt, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, and pepper.
  3. Add the egg and buttermilk and stir until combined. Stir in cubed butter.
  4. Drop the batter by rounded tspfuls in the hot oil.
  5. Let cook until they are golden brown 2-3 minutes. Remove with a metal strainer on a paper toweled lined plate.
  6. Serve with tartar sauce if desired.
