Desserts
Dirt cake is a no-bake dessert made with Oreo cookies, pudding, and whipped cream on top. The name of the dish refers to the fact that the cake has a rough, messy, and dirty look. Often, gummy worms are placed on top of the cake along with the whipped cream, acting as a decoration.
Meal prep time : 20 minutes
Servings : 10
Dirt Cake
Ingredients:
  • 32 ounces chocolate sandwich cookies with creme filling
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • ½ cup confectioners' suga
  • 3 ½ cups milk
  • 1 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 2 (3.5 ounce) packages instant vanilla pudding mix

Directions:
  1. Finely crush chocolate cookies in a food processor; set crumbs aside.
  2. Combine cream cheese, butter, and confectioner's sugar in a large bowl, beat with an electric mixer until smooth.
  3. Whisk milk, whipped topping, and pudding together in a separate large bowl, fold in cream cheese mixture until well combined.
  4. Alternately layer crushed cookies and pudding mixture in a medium flower pot, starting and ending with cookies.
  5. Chill dirt cake in the refrigerator until ready to serve; garnish as desired.
