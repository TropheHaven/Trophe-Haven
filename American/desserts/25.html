Peanut butter cookie consists of hand-rolled dough that is then flattened with fork tins, giving the cookies their characteristical waffle pattern. It is believed that the dough is marked to help cookies bake more evenly, but some claim it can also be a warning sign to people with peanut allergies. The dough usually consists of butter, peanut butter, eggs, milk, flour, sugar, and salt.
Meal prep time : 15 minutes
Servings : 18
Peanut Butter Cookie
Ingredients:
  • 1 ½ cup all-purpose flour 180g
  • ½ cup butter unsalted, room temp 113g
  • 1 cup peanut butter 250g
  • ½ cup brown sugar 100g, lightly packed
  • ½ cup sugar 100g
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract 5mL
  • 1 egg large, room temp
  • ¾ tsp baking powder 3g

Directions:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Sift flour and baking powder together then whisk to combine.
  3. Cream butter and sugars in a stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment. You can add an optional ¼-½ tsp sea salt.
  4. Add peanut butter and mix until incorporated.
  5. Mix in egg and vanilla extract then add flour mixture and beat until incorporated.
  6. Roll dough into one inch balls and place on baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  7. Flatten cookies with a fork in a criss-cross pattern.
  8. Bake cookies for about 10 minutes.
  9. Allow cookies to cool completely on baking sheet, they need to set up before being transferred.
