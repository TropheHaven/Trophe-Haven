Arroz chaufa is a popular Peruvian dish with a great influence of Chinese cuisine. It consists of a combination of fried rice, meats, vegetables, and fruits. Most commonly, rice is fried with shredded chicken, salami, garlic, ginger, slivered almonds, eggs, chicken stock, soy sauce, etc.
Meal prep time : 15 minutes
Servings : 5
Arroz Chaufa
Ingredients:
  • 2 Tbs sesame oil
  • 1 bunch green onion, chopped, (divided into greener and whiter parts)
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 hot dogs
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tablespoon ginger root , grated
  • 1 cup cooked and shredded chicken
  • 4 cups pre-cooked, cold rice
  • 2-3 Tablespoons soy sauce (more or less to taste)

Directions:
  1. Preheat a large skillet or wok to medium heat.
  2. Pour sesame oil in the bottom.
  3. Add hot dogs, onion, and bell pepper.
  4. Add garlic and ginger.
  5. Slide to the side, and pour the beaten eggs onto the other side.
  6. Using a spatula, scramble the eggs.
  7. Once cooked, mix the eggs with the vegetable mix.
  8. Add the rice and chicken to the veggie and egg mixture.
  9. Pour the soy sauce on top.
  10. Stir and fry the rice and veggie mixture until heated through and combined.
  11. Season with additional salt, if needed, to taste.
