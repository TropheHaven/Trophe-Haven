Drinks
The aromatic Blueberry Tea is an American cocktail made with a combination of hot tea and liqueurs. The cocktail is typically prepared with orange tea, almond liqueur, and orange liqueur mixed in a snifter glass that is used to hold the alcohol fumes which are released by the tea’s heat. If desired, the rim of the glass can be sugared, while the cocktail is often garnished with lemon slices. Although there are no blueberries in the drink, the name is derived from the cocktail’s fruity flavor, which is sometimes compared to blueberries.
Meal prep time : 5 minutes
Servings : 1
Blueberry Tea
Ingredients:
  • 1 fluid ounce amaretto liqueur
  • 1 fluid ounce orange liqueur (such as Grand Marnier®)
  • 4 fluid ounces orange pekoe tea, or more to taste

Directions:
  1. Combine amaretto and orange liqueurs in a brandy snifter or glass.
  2. Pour in hot tea.
