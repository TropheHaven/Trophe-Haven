Drinks
White Russian cocktail is made with a combination of vodka, cream, and coffee liqueur. To prepare it, vodka and coffee liqueur are poured into an old-fashioned glass over ice. Cream is then floated on top, and the whole combination is gently stirred with a straw. The drink is named after its key ingredient, vodka, which is typically associated with Russia. White Russian gained huge popularity in 1998, when the cult classic The Big Lebowski was released - the main character in the movie, called The Dude, drinks White Russians throughout the movie.
Meal prep time : 5 minutes
Servings : 1
White Russian
Ingredients:
  • 5 cl (50 ml) (1 2/3 oz) vodka
  • 2 cl (20 ml) (2/3 oz) coffee liqueur
  • cream, as desired (about 3 cl) (30 ml) (1 oz)

Directions:
  1. Fill an Old-Fashioned glass with ice, then add the vodka and the coffee liqueur, and stir.
  2. Float the cream over the ice, then carefully stir.
