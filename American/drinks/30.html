Drinks
Ranch Water is a light tequila cocktail hailing from Texas. It incorporates tequila, lime juice, and sparkling mineral water—preferably Topo Chico, which is ubiquitous in Texas. Some variations also opt for a splash of orange liqueur.
Meal prep time : 5 minutes
Servings : 1
Ranch Water
Ingredients:
  • 1 1/2 ounces blanco tequila
  • 1/2 ounce lime juice, freshly squeezed
  • Topo Chico, chilled, to top (about 6 ounces)
  • Garnish: lime wedge

Directions:
  1. Add all ingredients into a collins or highball glass over ice and stir briefly and gently to combine.
  2. Garnish with a lime wedge.
