Drinks
Mudslide is a popular American cocktail made with a combination of vodka, coffee liqueur (e.g. Kahlua), and cream liqueur (e.g. Baileys). In order to prepare it, all ingredients are shaken with ice, then strained into a glass filled with ice cubes. Alternatively, all ingredients can be blended together until smooth, poured into a glass, then drizzled with chocolate syrup, if desired. It is believed that the first Mudslide was prepared during the 1950s at the Wreck Bar in the Cayman Islands.
Meal prep time : 5 minutes
Servings : 1
Mudslide
Ingredients:
  • 1 ounce vodka
  • 1 ounce coffee liqueur
  • 1 ounce Baileys Irish cream
  • 1 1/2 ounces heavy cream
  • chocolate shavings

Directions:
  1. Add the vodka, coffee liqueur, Baileys and cream to a shaker with ice and shake until well-chilled.
  2. Strain into a chilled fizz glass or coupe.
  3. Garnish with freshly shaved chocolate.
